Homepage Concepts

Full-page prototypes exploring different strategic directions for the DreamHost homepage.

Concept A
Commercial / Price-Led

Plan cards front and center. Hosting pricing leads the page at $2.95/mo, followed by Remixer as the easy alternative. Performance data and reliability benchmarks close the deal. The Hostinger/GoDaddy playbook — price-first, trust-second — adapted for DreamHost.

Unlike competitors: DreamHost shows renewal pricing upfront (Hostinger hides 5.5x increases), leads with 0% failure rate data (no competitor publishes load test results), and positions Remixer as an honest alternative rather than forcing everyone through the same funnel.
Concept B
Remixer-Led

AI website builder as the headline. $1.99/mo Netflix pricing anchor. "Built with Remixer" gallery. Hosting plans positioned as the engine underneath. Target: the no-dashboard crowd who just wants to be online.

Unlike competitors: GoDaddy wraps everything under "Airo" branding but still requires a dashboard. Hostinger's AI is bolt-on. Remixer IS the product — describe what you want, watch it build. No competitor offers a truly conversational site builder at this price point.
Concept C
Social Proof / Trust-Led

Trustpilot in the hero, customer testimonials woven throughout, independence story front and center. Every section reinforces credibility — 29 years, 1.5M websites, 100% uptime, real performance benchmarks.

Unlike competitors: SiteGround puts Trustpilot 4.9 in the hero but stops there. DreamHost combines trust data with real performance benchmarks (0% failure rate) and 29 years of independence — a trust story no PE-owned competitor can tell.
Concept D
Performance-Led

Data IS the differentiator. Dark hero with 0% failure rate headline, benchmark comparison charts, TTFB speed bars, datacenter map. Every claim backed by independent ReviewSignal testing.

Unlike competitors: BlueHost publishes one number (247ms). Nobody else publishes any. DreamHost has K6 load test data showing 0% failure while WPEngine hits 13.5% and Kinsta hits 93.4%. This is the only concept where the data IS the differentiator, not just supporting evidence.
Concept E
Audience-Led (Small Business)

Organized around small business outcomes, not products. Persona cards lead the page — Local Shops, Online Sellers, Service Providers — followed by Remixer, Free Marketing Tools, Pro Services, and transparent pricing. Every section answers: "How does this help my business?"

Unlike competitors: No competitor explicitly targets small businesses on their homepage. DreamHost owns the vertical with tools, support, and messaging built for business owners — not generic "build a website" language.
Competitor Study
Hostinger-Style

Price-first conversion machine. Countdown timer in promo bar. Hero sells one price with "Claim Deal" CTA. Two-card product entry, stacked "From Idea to Online" showcase, customer gallery, press logos, and pricing table at the bottom. Dense, urgency-driven.

Unlike Hostinger: DreamHost shows renewal pricing upfront (Hostinger hides 5.5x increases) and offers a 30-Day Money-Back Guarantee. Same urgency format, honest pricing.
Competitor Study
BlueHost-Style

Trust stacking from the first pixel. Phone number in top bar. Hero with WordPress.org badge and trust signals. Editorial "Why DreamHost" feature blocks. Video testimonial. Speed benchmark chart with real TTFB data. Pricing buried deep after trust-building.

Unlike BlueHost: BlueHost publishes one speed number (247ms). DreamHost has K6 load test data, 0% failure rate, and 3 datacenter regions. Same trust-first structure, backed by data BlueHost can't match.
Competitor Study
SiteGround-Style

The leanest page. No pricing anywhere. Trustpilot directly in a dark hero. 6 equal-weight product cards — no hierarchy. AI platform showcase. "Real support, real humans 24/7" as a counter to AI-everything. Maximum whitespace, premium confidence.

Unlike SiteGround: SiteGround resells others' AI (ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini). DreamHost built Remixer — a first-party AI product. Same premium positioning, proprietary AI story.
Competitor Study
GoDaddy-Style

Domain search IS the hero. Tabbed product navigator lets visitors self-select into Domains, Websites, Hosting, Security, or Email. Unified "Remixer AI" branding across everything. Audience-identity cards. Phone support emphasis. 9-question FAQ for SEO.

Unlike GoDaddy: Same domain-first capture and product tabs, but without the bloat. Honest pricing, no "Airo every day" repetition, fewer sections selling everything to everyone.
